Information Architecture & User Experience Analysis

Detailed UX and IA layout explanations for the Neuro IT London platform.

Frictionless Deferred Authentication

Mandatory signup screens were moved to the end of the booking cycle. Users select services, postcodes, and describe issues anonymously. Draft state is cached locally, triggering login (Google OAuth / Passwordless OTP) right before ticket creation to minimize customer abandonment.

Cross-linking and Local SEO Siloing

To boost search rankings in London local search and Google Maps, the system features 33 dynamic landing pages for each London borough. Public services are cross-linked to relevant boroughs, displaying local pricing zones and congestion charges.

Tokenized Guest Access

To prevent friction from long sign-up processes, the system generates a unique security token for every booked ticket. Emailed to the customer, it allows guest tracking of status updates and feedback submission without credentials.

Smart AI Blog Autopilot with Gemini

The platform fetches tech RSS feeds from TechCrunch and VentureBeat, utilizing them as context for Gemini AI to draft fresh, SEO-optimised blog posts aligned with recent market trends.

Consolidation of Pricing and Services

The legacy /pricing page is permanently 301-redirected to /services for SEO value. All pricing data is merged directly into service catalogs to reduce user friction during booking.
